<p><p>The dental laboratory parallelometer market includes 1-arm and 2-arm models, essential for precise dental prosthesis fabrication. A 1-arm parallelometer features a single adjustable arm, offering simplicity and ease of use for basic tasks like surveying and measuring undercuts. In contrast, a 2-arm parallelometer has dual arms, providing enhanced stability and versatility for complex procedures, such as simultaneous measurements and intricate adjustments. Both types cater to dental technicians, with 1-arm models being cost-effective for routine work and 2-arm models preferred for advanced, high-precision applications.</p></p>
