<p><p>The aluminium die cut lids market is categorized into two main types based on alloy strength: soft aluminium alloys and half-hard aluminium alloys. Soft aluminium alloys are malleable and easily shaped, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and easy sealing. In contrast, half-hard aluminium alloys offer greater strength and rigidity, suitable for more demanding applications where durability and resistance to deformation are essential. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across industries, ensuring effective product protection and presentation.</p></p>
