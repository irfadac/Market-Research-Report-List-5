<p><p>The video dermatoscope market comprises two main types: traditional dermatoscopes and digital dermatoscopes. Traditional dermatoscopes are optical instruments typically using handheld magnification to examine skin lesions and conditions directly. In contrast, digital dermatoscopes feature a camera and software for image capture, enabling enhanced documentation, analysis, and remote consultations. Digital devices offer superior visualization, improved image storage, and teledermatology capabilities, making them increasingly popular among dermatologists and healthcare providers for more accurate diagnoses and patient management.</p></p>
