<p><p>The Mobile Hyperbaric Chamber market includes two primary types: Monoplace and Multiplace. Monoplace chambers are designed for a single patient, offering a compact, portable solution ideal for individual treatments, often used in clinics or home settings. Multiplace chambers accommodate multiple patients simultaneously, providing a larger, more versatile option suitable for hospitals or emergency scenarios. Both types deliver pressurized oxygen therapy, but Monoplace focuses on portability and simplicity, while Multiplace emphasizes capacity and shared treatment efficiency.</p></p>
