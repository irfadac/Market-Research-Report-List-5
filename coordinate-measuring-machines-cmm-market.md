<p><p>Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) are vital tools used in manufacturing and assembly processes to measure an object's physical geometrical characteristics. The market can be segmented into three primary types: </p><p>1. **Bridge CMM**: Known for high precision, these machines feature a bridge structure that supports the measuring probe, ideal for large components. </p><p>2. **Articulated Arm CMM**: Highly versatile and portable, these machines use a series of connected segments, allowing for quick measurements in various positions. </p><p>3. **Horizontal Arm CMM**: Positioned horizontally, these are suitable for large parts and heavy loads, offering stable measurement in industrial environments.</p></p>
