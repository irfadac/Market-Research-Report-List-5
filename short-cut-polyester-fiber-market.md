<p><p>The Short Cut Polyester Fiber market includes several types, each serving distinct applications. Blend fibers combine polyester with other materials for enhanced properties. Regular Shrink fibers are designed to shrink under heat, providing a soft feel. Low Shrink fibers maintain their original length with minimal shrinkage, ensuring stability in textiles. Undrawn (High Shrink) fibers expand significantly when heat-treated, creating bulkiness in fabrics. These variations cater to diverse consumer needs in the textiles and apparel industries, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
