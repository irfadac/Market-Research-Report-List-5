<p><strong>The Sports Mouthguard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preformed Mouthguard</li><li>Thermoformed Mouthguard</li><li>Custom Mouthguard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports mouthguard market comprises three main types: preformed, thermoformed, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are ready-to-use and offer basic protection, ideal for casual sports participants. Thermoformed mouthguards are molded by softening thermoplastic material in hot water, providing a better fit and comfort for athletes. Custom mouthguards are professionally crafted from dental impressions, ensuring maximum fit and protection tailored to individual needs, making them the preferred choice for serious athletes in high-contact sports.</p></p>
