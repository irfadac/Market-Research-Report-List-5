<p><strong>The Galvanized Iron (GI) Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Pipes</li><li>Welded Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized iron (GI) pipes market comprises two primary types: seamless pipes and welded pipes. Seamless pipes are produced from a solid round steel billet, offering enhanced strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for high-stress applications. On the other hand, welded pipes are manufactured by fusing steel sheets together, providing flexibility in sizes and thicknesses while being more cost-effective. Both types are widely used in construction, plumbing, and agricultural applications due to their durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
