<p><p>The manual strapping machine market is primarily divided into two types: welding and fastening strapping machines. Welding strapping machines use heat to bond plastic straps, providing a strong and durable seal for various applications, especially in packaging. In contrast, fastening strapping machines employ metal or plastic buckles to secure straps tightly, ideal for securing heavier loads. Both types are essential for enhancing packaging efficiency, ensuring product security during transportation, and catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
