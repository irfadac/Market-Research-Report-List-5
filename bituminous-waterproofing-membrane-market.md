<p><p>A bituminous waterproofing membrane is a durable, flexible material used to prevent water infiltration in construction projects. <p><strong>The Bituminous Waterproofing Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>APP (Atactic Polypropylene) Modified</li><li>SBS Modified</li><li>Self-Adhesive Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bituminous Waterproofing Membrane market includes three main types: APP (Atactic Polypropylene) Modified, SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) Modified, and Self-Adhesive Membrane. APP membranes are heat-applied, offering UV resistance and durability, ideal for exposed surfaces. SBS membranes are flexible, cold-applied, and excel in crack-bridging, suitable for areas with movement. Self-Adhesive membranes feature a pre-applied adhesive layer, enabling quick, easy installation without heat or solvents, making them convenient for small-scale projects. Each type caters to specific waterproofing needs based on application, climate, and structural requirements.</p></p>
