<p><p>The automotive intelligent headlight market encompasses advanced lighting technologies designed to enhance visibility and safety while driving. The Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) adjusts the headlight beam's direction based on the vehicle's speed, steering angle, and road conditions, improving illumination around corners. The Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB) utilizes multiple light sources to modify the headlight pattern dynamically, providing optimal lighting without blinding oncoming drivers. Both systems significantly enhance nighttime driving experiences and road safety.</p></p>
