<p><strong>The Solar Electric Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar Electric Propulsion Systems market encompasses several advanced technologies. Hall Effect Thrusters (HET) utilize electric fields to accelerate ions, offering high efficiency and thrust for long-duration space missions. Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT) generate thrust by discharging short pulses of plasma, suitable for small satellites due to their simplicity and low power requirements. Other propulsion technologies include ion thrusters and arcjet systems, providing versatile options for various space applications, focused on enhancing propulsion performance and efficiency in missions.</p></p>
