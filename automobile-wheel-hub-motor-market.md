<p><p>The automobile wheel hub motor market comprises two main types: inner-rotor and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors feature the rotor positioned inside the stator, allowing for a compact design with a better weight distribution and enhanced torque. Conversely, external-rotor motors have the rotor on the outside, offering a larger surface area for heat dissipation and improved cooling efficiency. Both designs aim to enhance electric vehicle performance and efficiency, catering to the growing demand for sustainable mobility solutions.</p></p>
