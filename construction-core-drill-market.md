<p><ul><li>Wet Drill Bits</li><li>Dry Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction core drill market primarily features two types of drill bits: wet and dry. Wet drill bits utilize a coolant, often water, to reduce heat and prolong bit life while efficiently removing debris during the drilling process, making them ideal for hard materials. In contrast, dry drill bits operate without coolants, generating more dust but allowing for faster drilling in softer materials. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to specific project requirements and environmental conditions within the construction industry.</p></p>
