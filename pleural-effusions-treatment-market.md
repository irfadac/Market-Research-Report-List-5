<p><ul><li>Thoracentesis</li><li>Pleurodesis</li><li>Pleuroperitoneal Shunt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pleural effusions treatment market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Thoracentesis** involves the needle drainage of excess fluid from the pleural cavity, offering immediate relief and diagnostic benefits. </p><p>2. **Pleurodesis** is a procedure that aims to obliterate the pleural space by causing the pleurae to stick together, preventing future fluid accumulation. </p><p>3. **Pleuroperitoneal shunt** involves creating a passage between the pleural cavity and the peritoneal cavity to facilitate continuous drainage of excess pleural fluid, thus managing chronic effusions effectively.</p></p>
