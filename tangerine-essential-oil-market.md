<p><p>The Tangerine Essential Oil market primarily features two types: Absolute and Blends. Absolute tangerine oil is derived through a solvent extraction process that captures the full aromatic profile of the fruit, resulting in a rich, potent oil used in perfumery and cosmetics. Conversely, blends combine tangerine oil with other essential oils or carrier oils, enhancing therapeutic properties and fragrance complexity. These blends cater to diverse applications, including aromatherapy, personal care, and household products, appealing to a wide range of consumers.</p></p>
