<p><strong>The Heat Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat pump market is primarily divided into two types: air-to-air and air-to-water systems. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat between the outside air and the indoor air, efficiently heating or cooling spaces. They are ideal for residential applications. Conversely, air-to-water heat pumps extract heat from the air to warm water, suitable for heating water in radiators or underfloor heating systems. Both systems are increasingly popular for their energy efficiency and reducing carbon footprints in heating solutions.</p></p>
