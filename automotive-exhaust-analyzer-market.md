<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust analyzer market features various technologies used to measure emissions. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers detect gases like CO2 and CO by measuring infrared light absorption. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) analyze hydrocarbons by burning the sample and measuring ionized particles. Chemiluminescence analyzers assess nitrogen oxides through light emitted during chemical reactions. Constant Volume Samplers measure exhaust in a controlled volume for accurate analysis of pollutants. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
