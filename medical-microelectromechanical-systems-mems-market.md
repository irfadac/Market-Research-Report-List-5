<p><p>Medical Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS) encompass various sensor types crucial for diagnostics and patient monitoring. Pressure sensors measure physiological pressure changes, while optical sensors detect light variations for imaging and diagnostics. Infrared (IR) sensors are used for temperature monitoring and disease detection. Flow sensors track fluid movement, essential for drug delivery systems. Microfluidic device sensors manage small fluid volumes for assays and diagnostics. Other sensors include biosensors and accelerometers, each enhancing medical applications through miniaturization and precision.</p></p>
