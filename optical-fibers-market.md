<p><strong>The Optical Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fibers market is primarily categorized into two types: Multi-Mode Fiber Optics and Single-Mode Fiber Optics. Multi-Mode Fiber Optics utilize multiple light paths within the fiber, suitable for short-distance communication, such as within buildings, offering higher bandwidth over shorter distances. Conversely, Single-Mode Fiber Optics allows only one light path, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for telecommunications and internet backbone connections. Both types play crucial roles in modern data transmission systems.</p></p>
