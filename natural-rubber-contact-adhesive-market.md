<p><p>The Natural Rubber Contact Adhesive market consists of two primary types: solvent-based adhesives and waterborne adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents which provide strong bonding and are ideal for heavy-duty applications, offering quick drying times. However, they can emit volatile organic compounds. In contrast, waterborne adhesives use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC emissions. They are suitable for less demanding applications and are increasingly favored for their safer handling and ease of cleanup.</p></p>
