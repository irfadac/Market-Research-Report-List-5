<p><p>The Oxidation Reduction Potential (ORP) electrode market is primarily categorized into single junction and double junction types. Single junction electrodes consist of a single solution interface, making them simpler and more cost-effective, but less stable in contaminated environments. In contrast, double junction electrodes feature a secondary junction that provides additional protection against sample interference, enhancing longevity and accuracy. These types cater to diverse applications, with double junctions often preferred in complex or industrial environments requiring greater reliability.</p></p>
