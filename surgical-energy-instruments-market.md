<p><p>The surgical energy instruments market comprises several types that enhance surgical procedures through precise tissue manipulation. Monopolar instruments use a single electrode for cutting and coagulating tissue, ideal for various surgeries. Bipolar instruments utilize two electrodes to control energy flow, reducing collateral damage and improving safety. Ultrasonic instruments leverage high-frequency sound waves for cutting while simultaneously coagulating, minimizing bleeding. Other devices in this market include advanced technologies such as laser and radiofrequency devices, broadening options for surgeons and hospitals.</p></p>
