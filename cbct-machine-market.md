<p><p>The Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) machine market is divided into two primary types based on the field of view (FOV): large FOV and medium FOV. Large FOV CBCT machines provide a broader imaging area, making them suitable for comprehensive scans of the head, neck, and entire dental arch, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. Medium FOV machines, on the other hand, are more focused, ideal for specific applications such as single tooth assessments or localized regions, offering detailed images while minimizing radiation exposure.</p></p>
