<p><strong>The Membrane Filtration Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li><li>Nanofiltration</li><li>Reverse Osmosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane filtration apparatus market is segmented into four main types: microfiltration, ultrafiltration, nanofiltration, and reverse osmosis. Microfiltration separates larger particles and microorganisms, while ultrafiltration targets smaller solutes, including macromolecules and colloids. Nanofiltration operates on even finer scales, allowing selective ion removal and softening of water. Reverse osmosis is the most advanced, effectively removing dissolved salts and contaminants by applying high pressure. Each technology serves unique applications across water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing industries.</p></p>
