<p><p>The HVAC valves market comprises several types, including ball valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, and others. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and minimal flow resistance, making them ideal for on/off applications. Globe valves provide precise flow control and are suitable for regulating fluid flow. Butterfly valves are lightweight and efficient, commonly used for throttling and regulating in large pipelines. Other valves may include check, solenoid, or pressure relief valves, catering to specialized applications within HVAC systems for improved efficiency and performance.</p></p>
