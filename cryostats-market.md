<p><ul><li>Bath Cryostats</li><li>Closed-Cycle Cryostats</li><li>Multistage Cryostats</li><li>Continuous-Flow Cryostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryostats are vital tools for maintaining low temperatures in various research and industrial applications. Bath cryostats utilize a refrigerant-filled reservoir to achieve desired cooling, while closed-cycle cryostats employ a closed system for continuous operation without the need for liquid nitrogen. Multistage cryostats combine different cooling stages for ultra-low temperatures, and continuous-flow cryostats facilitate a steady flow of cryogenic liquids, ensuring consistent cooling without interruption. Each type serves specific needs in scientific research, materials testing, and semiconductor processing.</p></p>
