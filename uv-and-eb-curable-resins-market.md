<p><ul><li>Radical Curable Resin</li><li>Cationic Curable Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV and EB curable resins market is characterized by two main types: radical curable resins and cationic curable resins. Radical curable resins utilize free radicals to initiate polymerization upon exposure to ultraviolet light or electron beams, resulting in quick curing and strong adhesion. In contrast, cationic curable resins rely on cationic polymerization, which is often more tolerant to moisture and produces high-performance coatings. Both types are widely used in industries like printing, coatings, adhesives, and textiles, offering enhanced efficiency and durability.</p></p>
