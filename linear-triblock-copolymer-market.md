<p><strong>The Linear Triblock Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Anionic Polymerization</li><li>Inactive Anionic Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear triblock copolymer market is categorized into active and inactive anionic polymerization types. Active anionic polymerization involves the use of anionic initiators that provide control over molecular weight and architecture, leading to well-defined block structures. In contrast, inactive anionic polymerization lacks such effective initiators, resulting in less precise control but potentially simpler processing. Both types cater to various applications, including adhesives, coatings, and elastomers, impacting the performance and properties of the resulting triblock copolymers.</p></p>
