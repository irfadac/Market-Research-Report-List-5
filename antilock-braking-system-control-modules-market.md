<p><p>Antilock Braking System (ABS) control modules are categorized based on the number of channels they use to monitor and control wheel speed. One-channel ABS is primarily designed for single-axle vehicles, offering basic functionality. Two-channel ABS provides control for front wheels, enhancing safety during braking. Three-channel ABS adds monitoring for a rear wheel, optimizing performance in larger vehicles. Four-channel ABS offers independent control for all wheels, delivering superior stability and safety, making it ideal for modern cars with advanced braking needs.</p></p>
