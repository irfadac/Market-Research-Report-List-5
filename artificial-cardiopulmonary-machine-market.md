<p><strong>The Artificial Cardiopulmonary Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump HLM</li><li>Double Roller Pump HLM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial cardiopulmonary machine market primarily consists of two types: single roller pump heart-lung machines (HLM) and double roller pump HLMs. Single roller pump HLMs utilize a single roller mechanism to circulate blood, offering simplicity and ease of use, making them suitable for various surgical procedures. Conversely, double roller pump HLMs feature two rollers, providing enhanced flow control and increased efficiency, which is crucial for complex surgeries requiring more precise blood management. Both types play essential roles in cardiac surgery and critical care.</p></p>
