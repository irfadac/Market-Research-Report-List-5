<p><p>The polyurethane elastic sealant market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component sealants. One-component polyurethane sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, two-component polyurethane sealants require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, providing enhanced performance and durability. Both types are widely used in construction and automotive industries for sealing joints, gaps, and surfaces, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility.</p></p>
