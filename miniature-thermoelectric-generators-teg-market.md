<p><p>Miniature Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs) are compact devices that convert temperature differences into electrical energy. The market for TEGs is categorized into two main types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric element for heat conversion, making them simpler and cost-effective for low-temperature applications. In contrast, multistage modules consist of stacked thermoelectric elements, enabling higher efficiency and power output, suitable for applications requiring greater energy generation from significant temperature gradients.</p></p>
