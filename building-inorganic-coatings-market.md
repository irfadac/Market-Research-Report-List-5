<p><p>The building inorganic coatings market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Water-soluble coatings** are environmentally friendly, offering easy application and fast drying, suitable for various substrates. </p><p>2. **Solvent coatings** provide durable finishes and excellent adhesion but have higher VOC emissions, often used in industrial and commercial settings. </p><p>3. **Emulsion coatings** combine water and emulsifiers, delivering good flexibility and resistance to weathering, commonly utilized for exterior surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
