<p><ul><li>Multi-axis</li><li>Angular</li><li>Linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive position sensors market encompasses three primary types: multi-axis, angular, and linear sensors. Multi-axis sensors detect movement and position across multiple dimensions, making them ideal for advanced vehicle dynamics and stability control. Angular sensors measure the rotation of components, such as steering wheels or throttle pedals, enhancing vehicle response and control. Linear sensors track the straight-line position of various elements, such as brake and accelerator pedals, ensuring precise control in vehicle operations and improving safety and performance.</p></p>
