<p><p>The Substrate Support Mat market is divided into two main types: Intumescent and Non-Intumescent support mats. Intumescent substrate support mats expand upon exposure to heat, providing additional fire resistance and insulation, making them suitable for high-risk environments. Non-Intumescent substrate support mats, on the other hand, maintain their form and properties under heat without expanding, ensuring stability in various applications. Both types serve critical roles in construction and industrial settings, enhancing safety and structural integrity.</p></p>
