<p><p>The residential epoxy floor coatings market is categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based epoxy offers low VOC emissions, making it ideal for indoor use, while solvent-based coatings provide strong adhesion and durability, suitable for high-traffic areas. On the other hand, 100% solids epoxy contains no solvents, leading to superior strength and a thicker application, making it perfect for heavy-duty residential environments. Each type caters to specific preferences and flooring needs.</p></p>
