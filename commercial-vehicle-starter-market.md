<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle starter market can be categorized into two main types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters operate through a mechanical linkage, providing immediate engagement with the engine. This ensures reliable starting in various conditions. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters utilize an electromagnetic system to engage the starter motor, offering more precise control and reducing wear. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring efficient engine starting, but they vary in mechanics, response, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
