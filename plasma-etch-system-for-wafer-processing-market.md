<p><ul><li>Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP)</li><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE)</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma etch system for wafer processing includes three main market types: Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP), Reactive Ion Etching (RIE), and Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE). ICP utilizes electromagnetic fields to create highly ionized plasma for precise etching, suitable for advanced microfabrication. RIE combines both chemical and physical etching, offering improved feature resolution. DRIE allows for deep and vertical etching, essential for creating high aspect ratio structures in MEMS and other advanced applications, making it crucial for semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
