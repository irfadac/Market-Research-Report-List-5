Stackers are designed to create stockpiles of bulk materials by stacking them in designated areas. Reclaimers, on the other hand, are utilized to retrieve stored materials from these stockpiles for processing or transportation. Combined Stacker Reclaimers integrate both functionalities, enabling efficient material handling by simultaneously stacking and reclaiming. Each type serves specific operational needs in industries like mining, construction, and port management.</p></p>
