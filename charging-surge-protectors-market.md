<p><strong>The Charging Surge Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Voltage Limited Type</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Charging surge protectors are categorized into three main types based on their operational mechanism. The Voltage Switch Type activates when a surge exceeds a preset voltage, diverting excess energy away. The Voltage Limited Type restricts the voltage level, allowing only safe levels to pass through to the connected device. The Combination Type integrates features of both previous types, providing robust protection against surges while maintaining energy flow. Each type serves to safeguard electronic devices from damaging voltage spikes.</p></p>
