<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>M Type</li><li>B Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Echo-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main types: A-Type, M-Type, and B-Type. A-Type provides amplitude information, presenting signals as spikes on a display, useful for measuring distances. M-Type, or motion mode, captures the movement of structures over time, making it ideal for cardiac assessments. B-Type, or brightness mode, generates 2D images by measuring the intensity of reflections, offering detailed visualization of internal organs and tissues. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes in medical imaging.</p></p>
