<p><p>Cationic UV-curable resins are specialized materials used in coatings, adhesives, and inks that cure when exposed to ultraviolet light. Two primary types are epoxy resins and vinyl ether resins. Epoxy resins offer excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, vinyl ether resins provide faster curing times and flexibility, suited for applications where quick processing and elasticity are essential. Both types contribute significantly to industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
