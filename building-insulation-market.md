<p><p>The building insulation market comprises several types, each with distinct properties. Stone or rock wool offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation and is fire-resistant, making it suitable for various applications. Glass wool, made from recycled glass, provides good thermal insulation and is lightweight, commonly used in residential and commercial buildings. Expanded polystyrene (EPS) and extruded polystyrene (XPS) are lightweight foam materials that deliver high thermal resistance and moisture resistance, ideal for insulation in walls and roofs. Other materials may include cellulose and spray foam, catering to specific insulation needs.</p></p>
