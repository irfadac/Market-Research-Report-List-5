<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper market is categorized into two main types: Single Piston Calipers and Multi-Piston Calipers. Single Piston Calipers utilize one piston to apply braking force, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for lighter commercial vehicles. In contrast, Multi-Piston Calipers feature multiple pistons, providing enhanced braking performance, faster response times, and improved heat dissipation. These are typically used in heavier vehicles where greater stopping power and reliability are essential for safety and efficiency in demanding operating conditions.</p></p>
