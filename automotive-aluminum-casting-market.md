<p><p>The automotive aluminum casting market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Die Casting** is a high-pressure process producing precise, complex parts with excellent surface finish, often used for high-volume production.  </p><p>2. **Permanent Mold Casting** utilizes reusable molds, offering good dimensional accuracy and surface quality, ideal for medium production runs.  </p><p>3. **Sand Casting** involves creating molds from sand, allowing for large, intricate components but with less precision and finish than die casting.  </p><p>4. **Others** may include techniques like investment casting and squeeze casting, catering to specialized applications in the automotive sector.</p></p>
