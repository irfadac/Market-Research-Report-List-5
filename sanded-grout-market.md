<p><ul><li>Finely Sanded Grout</li><li>Quarry Type Grout</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sanded grout market includes two main types: finely sanded grout and quarry-type grout. Finely sanded grout contains finer particles that provide a smooth finish, making it suitable for larger joints, typically over 1/8 inch, while preventing shrinkage and cracking. Quarry-type grout, on the other hand, is coarser and designed for heavier applications such as setting tiles in wet areas or for exterior use. Both types enhance durability and adhesion in flooring and wall installations, catering to various construction needs.</p></p>
