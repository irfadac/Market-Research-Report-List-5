<p><p>The Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) System market comprises several types based on design and functionality. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed for long-range and endurance flights, ideal for surveillance and mapping. Rotary-wing UAVs, or drones, offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, perfect for short-range tasks and agile maneuvers. Flapping-wing UAVs mimic bird flight, enhancing efficiency in specific environments. Airship UAVs combine buoyancy and propulsion for extended flight times, mainly used in surveillance and communications. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
