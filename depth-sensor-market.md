<p><strong>The Depth Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Sensing</li><li>Passive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depth sensor market is categorized into two main types: active sensing and passive sensing. Active sensing involves emitting signals, such as infrared light or ultrasound, to measure distances by analyzing the reflected signals, enabling precise depth measurement in various lighting conditions. In contrast, passive sensing relies on ambient light or existing environmental data to capture depth information, often used in applications like computer vision and augmented reality. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industries, including automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
