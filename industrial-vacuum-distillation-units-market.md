<p><p>Industrial vacuum distillation units are categorized into two main types: dry type and wet type. Dry type units operate under vacuum conditions without the presence of liquid, allowing for the distillation of high-boiling point substances and improving efficiency by reducing thermal degradation. In contrast, wet type units utilize a liquid phase during the distillation process, enabling the separation of components with closer boiling points. Both types serve distinct applications in various industries, ensuring effective separation and purification of chemical products.</p></p>
