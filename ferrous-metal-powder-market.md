<p><ul><li>Cold Compaction</li><li>Hot Compaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ferrous metal powder market is primarily categorized into two compaction methods: cold compaction and hot compaction. Cold compaction involves compressing metal powders at room temperature, resulting in precise shapes and sizes with enhanced surface finish, ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, hot compaction applies heat to improve flow properties and facilitate denser packing of powders, often leading to superior mechanical properties in the final product. Both methods cater to different manufacturing needs in industries like automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
