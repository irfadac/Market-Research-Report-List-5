<p><p>Floating Production Systems (FPS) are advanced offshore platforms designed for the extraction and processing of hydrocarbons. The market includes several types: FPSOs (Floating Production Storage and Offloading units) are versatile vessels that combine production and storage capabilities. Tension Leg Platforms (TLPs) are anchored to the seabed with taut tendons, offering stability in deep waters. Spar platforms are anchored with long chains or tendons, ideal for deep-water locations. Barges are simpler, more cost-effective options often used for shallow water operations. Each type serves specific operational needs in the offshore oil and gas industry.</p></p>
