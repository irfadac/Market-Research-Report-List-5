In contrast, multi-spark coils produce multiple sparks, enhancing combustion efficiency and improving engine performance. Multi-spark systems are gaining popularity in performance-driven and modern applications, as they offer better fuel economy and reduced emissions. The choice between the two types often depends on the specific performance requirements and technological advancements in the automotive sector.</p></p>
