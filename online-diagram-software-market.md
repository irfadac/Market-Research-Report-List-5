<p><p>The online diagram software market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access their diagrams and collaborate in real-time from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically offers subscription-based pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's device or network, providing greater control over data security and customization but may involve higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
