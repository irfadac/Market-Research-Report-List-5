<p><p>Hotel Revenue Management Systems (RMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based RMS solutions operate through internet connectivity, allowing hotels to access real-time analytics and updates from any device, resulting in lower upfront costs and easier scalability. In contrast, on-premises RMS are installed locally within a hotel's infrastructure, offering more control over data and customization but requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options aim to optimize pricing and maximize revenue.</p></p>
