<p><p>The Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) Safety Modem market is segmented into Active and Passive DSRC types. Active DSRC utilizes real-time communication between vehicles and infrastructure for safety applications, enhancing situational awareness and enabling immediate responses to hazards. In contrast, Passive DSRC focuses on data collection and analysis without real-time interaction, primarily used for traffic monitoring and research purposes. Together, these two types improve road safety and traffic management through different approaches to information exchange and utilization.</p></p>
