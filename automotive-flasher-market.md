<p><strong>The Automotive Flasher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Flasher</li><li>Thermal Flasher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flasher market includes two main types: electronic and thermal flashers. Electronic flashers use solid-state components to control turn signal timing, offering precise operation, durability, and compatibility with LED lights. They are energy-efficient and widely used in modern vehicles. Thermal flashers rely on a bimetallic strip that heats and cools to create the flashing effect. They are simpler, cost-effective, and commonly found in older vehicles but are less efficient and incompatible with LEDs. Both serve to regulate turn signal functionality in vehicles.</p></p>
