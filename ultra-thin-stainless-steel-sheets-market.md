<p><p>The ultra-thin stainless steel sheets market is categorized into cold-rolled and hot-rolled types. Cold-rolled sheets are produced by refining hot-rolled sheets at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface and tighter tolerances, making them ideal for precision applications. In contrast, hot-rolled sheets are manufactured at elevated temperatures, which makes them easier to shape but results in a rougher surface. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, based on their specific mechanical and aesthetic properties.</p></p>
