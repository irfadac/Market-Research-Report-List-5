<p><strong>The Rectangular Hollow Section Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WeldedRHS</li><li>SeamlessRHS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rectangular Hollow Section (RHS) steel market primarily consists of two types: Welded RHS and Seamless RHS. Welded RHS is produced by welding flat steel plates into shape, making it cost-effective and available in large sizes, suitable for construction and structural applications. Seamless RHS, on the other hand, is manufactured from solid round billets, providing higher strength and uniformity, ideal for applications requiring high pressure or load-bearing capabilities. Both types serve diverse industrial needs, enhancing structural integrity.</p></p>
