<p><p>The Vacuum Carbonization Furnace market is categorized into two main types: normal pressure and high pressure. In contrast, high-pressure furnaces use elevated pressures to enhance the carbonization process, resulting in improved material properties and faster processing times, making them ideal for advanced carbon materials. Both types cater to diverse industries, including waste treatment, materials science, and renewable energy.</p></p>
