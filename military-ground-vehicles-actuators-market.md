<p><p>The Military Ground Vehicles Actuators Market is categorized into two main types: linear and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert rotational motion into straight-line motion, essential for applications like weapon elevation or hatch operations in military vehicles. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, facilitate rotational movement, powering components such as turret rotation or steering mechanisms. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the functionality, maneuverability, and operational efficiency of military ground vehicles, ensuring they meet demanding tactical requirements.</p></p>
