<p><strong>The Biosafety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biosafety cabinet market comprises three main types: Class II A, Class II B, and Class III. Class II A cabinets provide operator protection while allowing inward airflow and clean air to be directed toward the work area. Class II B cabinets offer enhanced protection with higher exhaust airflow and are suitable for volatile substances. Class III cabinets ensure maximum containment, designed for handling hazardous materials with a sealed environment, operated through gloves or robotic arms, ideal for high-risk scenarios.</p></p>
