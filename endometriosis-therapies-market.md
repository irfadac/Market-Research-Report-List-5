<p><p>Endometriosis therapies encompass a range of treatments aimed at managing the symptoms of endometriosis, a condition where tissue similar to the uterine lining grows outside the uterus, causing pain and infertility. Therapies include hormonal treatments, pain management medications, surgical interventions, and lifestyle modifications. Hormonal therapies, such as oral contraceptives, GnRH agonists, and progestins, are commonly used to regulate or suppress estrogen production, which fuels endometrial growth. Surgical options, including laparoscopy, aim to remove endometrial lesions and adhesions. Emerging therapies focus on targeted drug development and minimally invasive techniques to improve patient outcomes.</p><p>The Endometriosis Therapies Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing awareness, rising prevalence of endometriosis, and advancements in treatment options. <p><p>The **Endometriosis Therapies Market** includes various treatment types targeting symptom management and disease progression. **Hormonal Contraceptives** regulate menstrual cycles, reducing pain and endometrial growth. **Gonadotropin-releasing Hormone (Gn-RH) Agonists** suppress estrogen production, shrinking endometrial tissue. **Progestin Therapy** inhibits ovulation and reduces tissue growth by stabilizing hormone levels. **Aromatase Inhibitors** block estrogen synthesis, limiting endometrial tissue proliferation. These therapies aim to alleviate pain, improve quality of life, and slow disease progression, catering to diverse patient needs and severity levels.</p></p>
