<p><p>Heating and cooling ceiling systems are designed to provide thermal comfort by distributing temperatures evenly across a space. The market comprises two main types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating systems utilize radiators or hydronic tubes, circulating warm water to radiate heat, offering energy efficiency and quiet operation. In contrast, electric heating systems directly convert electricity into heat via cables or radiant panels, enabling rapid temperature adjustments and installation flexibility. Both systems cater to diverse residential and commercial needs.</p></p>
