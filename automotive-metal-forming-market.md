<p><p>The automotive metal forming market encompasses various techniques to shape metal components. **Roll Forming** involves bending metal sheets into desired profiles using rollers. **Stretch Forming** stretches metal over a die to achieve complex shapes. **Stamping** uses dies to cut or shape metal sheets into parts. **Deep Drawing** creates hollow components by drawing metal into a die. **Hydroforming** uses high-pressure fluid to mold metal into intricate shapes. **Others** include methods like forging and extrusion, catering to diverse automotive manufacturing needs.</p></p>
