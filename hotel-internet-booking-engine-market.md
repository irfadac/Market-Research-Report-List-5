<p><p>The Hotel Internet Booking Engine market can be categorized into cloud-based and web-based systems. This ensures scalability and automatic updates. In contrast, web-based booking engines are integrated directly into hotel websites, facilitating direct bookings. Both types enhance customer experience, increase online visibility, and streamline operations for hotels, making it easier to manage reservations and maximize occupancy.</p></p>
