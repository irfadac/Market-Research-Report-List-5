<p><p>Healthcare Clinical Trial Management Systems (CTMS) can be categorized into three main types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise solutions. Web-based CTMS allows users to access the system through a web browser, facilitating remote collaboration. Cloud-based CTMS offers scalability and flexibility, enabling real-time data sharing and reduced IT maintenance. On-premise CTMS, installed locally on company servers, provides greater control over data security and customization, but often involves higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Each type caters to different organizational needs.</p></p>
