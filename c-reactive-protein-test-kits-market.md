<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test kits market comprises various types, including immunoturbidimetric, ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay), and other methods. Immunoturbidimetric tests measure the cloudiness resulting from CRP-antibody interactions, while ELISA employs enzyme-linked antibodies for quantification. CLIA utilizes chemiluminescence to detect CRP levels, offering high sensitivity and speed. Other methods may include rapid tests or alternative immunoassay techniques, catering to diverse clinical needs and laboratory settings in monitoring inflammation and infections.</p></p>
