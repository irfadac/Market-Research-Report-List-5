<p><p>The Phase Change Materials (PCM) market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Organic Phase Change Materials** are typically based on hydrocarbons, offering a high latent heat capacity, non-toxic properties, and thermal stability. </p><p>2. **Inorganic Phase Change Materials** consist of salts and salt hydrates, known for their high heat storage capacity but may face issues like phase separation.</p><p>3. **Bio-based Phase Change Materials** are derived from natural sources, combining sustainability with effective thermal management, appealing to the eco-conscious market. Each type finds diverse applications in building energy efficiency, temperature regulation, and thermal storage.</p></p>
