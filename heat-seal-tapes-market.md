<p><p>The heat seal tapes market comprises three main types: PTFE, TPU, and PI. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) tapes are known for their high thermal resistance and low friction, making them ideal for applications in extreme temperatures. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) tapes offer flexibility and durability, suitable for packaging and clothing applications. PI (Polyimide) tapes exhibit excellent insulation properties, often used in electronics and aerospace. Each type serves distinct industries based on their unique thermal and mechanical properties, catering to specific sealing needs.</p></p>
