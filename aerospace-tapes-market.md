Acrylic tapes are known for their excellent bonding capabilities and resistance to UV light, making them suitable for exterior applications. Rubber tapes offer superior adhesion and flexibility, ideal for sealing and insulating components. Silicone tapes are highly resistant to extreme temperatures and chemicals, ensuring durability in challenging conditions.
