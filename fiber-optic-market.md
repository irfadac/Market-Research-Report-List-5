<p><p>The fiber optic market comprises various connector types, each catering to specific applications. FC connectors offer low insertion loss and are ideal for high-vibration environments. SC connectors provide a snap-in design for easy installation, making them popular in data center applications. LC connectors are compact and suitable for high-density environments, frequently used in telecom networks. ST connectors, known for their bayonet coupling, are commonly used in multi-mode fiber applications, providing secure connections in various networking scenarios.</p></p>
