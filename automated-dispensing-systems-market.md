<p><strong>The Automated Dispensing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems</li><li>De-Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Dispensing Systems (ADS) are categorized into centralized and decentralized types. Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems operate from a single location, managing inventory and dispensing medications directly to various departments within a healthcare facility, enhancing efficiency and oversight. In contrast, Decentralized Automated Dispensing Systems are distributed throughout different care areas, allowing for faster access to medications at the point of care. This decentralization increases medication availability and responsiveness, improving overall patient care and safety within healthcare settings.</p></p>
