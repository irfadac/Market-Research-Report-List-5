<p><ul><li>Long-side Terminal</li><li>Wraparound Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMD metal foil precision resistors are offered in two main types based on terminal design: long-side terminal and wraparound terminal. Long-side terminal resistors feature terminals that extend along one side, facilitating stable soldering and compact layouts on circuit boards. In contrast, wraparound terminal resistors have terminals that wrap around the resistor body, enhancing mechanical stability and thermal performance. Both types ensure high accuracy and low temperature coefficients, making them essential for applications demanding precise resistance values in various electronic devices.</p></p>
