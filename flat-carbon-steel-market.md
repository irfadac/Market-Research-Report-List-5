<p><p>The flat carbon steel market comprises three main types: low carbon, medium carbon, and high carbon steel. Low carbon steel, with up to 0.3% carbon, is malleable and commonly used in construction and automotive applications. Medium carbon steel, containing 0.3% to 0.6% carbon, offers improved strength and wear resistance, making it suitable for machinery and tools. High carbon steel, exceeding 0.6% carbon, is very strong and hard, ideal for cutting tools and high-stress applications, though it is more brittle.</p></p>
