<p><p>The dust-free paper market encompasses three primary types: latex-bonded airlaid paper, thermal-bonded airlaid paper, and multi-bonded airlaid paper. Latex-bonded airlaid paper utilizes latex to enhance bonding and improve strength, making it suitable for hygiene products. Thermal-bonded airlaid paper employs heat for bonding fibers, offering a soft texture and absorbency for applications like wipes. Multi-bonded airlaid paper combines both techniques, providing versatility and durability for various uses, including medical and personal care products, ensuring a high-performance standard across applications.</p></p>
