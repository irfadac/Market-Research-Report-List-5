<p><p>The radar market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Bistatic radar utilizes separate transmitter and receiver locations, enhancing surveillance capabilities. Continuous-wave radar offers real-time tracking by constantly transmitting signals. Doppler radar measures velocity changes, ideal for weather and aviation. Frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FM-CW) radar provides high-resolution distance measurement. Monopulse radar achieves precise angle measurement through simultaneous signal reception. Other radar types include pulse-Doppler and phase array radars, each designed for specialized functions across defense, aviation, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
