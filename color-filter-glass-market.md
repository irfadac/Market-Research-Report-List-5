<p><strong>The Color Filter Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass</li><li>Bandpass</li><li>Shortpass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Color Filter Glass market is segmented into three main types: Longpass, Bandpass, and Shortpass filters. Longpass filters transmit longer wavelengths while blocking shorter ones, making them ideal for applications requiring specific color separation. Bandpass filters allow a particular range of wavelengths to pass through while filtering out others, suitable for precision applications in imaging and optics. Shortpass filters, conversely, allow shorter wavelengths and block longer ones, facilitating various analytical and optical processes in scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
