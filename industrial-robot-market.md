<p><p>The industrial robot market encompasses various types, each designed for specific applications. Articulated robots, with their multi-jointed arms, excel in complex tasks like welding and assembly. Parallel robots, known for their stability and speed, are ideal for applications requiring precision. SCARA robots specialize in horizontal movements, making them suitable for tasks like pick-and-place. Cylindrical robots utilize a rotational base for flexible operations, while Cartesian robots, with linear movements on three axes, are used in packaging and material handling. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
