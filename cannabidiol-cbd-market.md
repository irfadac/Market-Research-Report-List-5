<p><p>The cannabidiol (CBD) market consists of two primary types: hemp-derived and marijuana-derived. Hemp-derived CBD is extracted from industrial hemp, which contains less than 0.3% THC, making it legal in many regions. This type is widely used in various wellness products due to its non-psychoactive nature. In contrast, marijuana-derived CBD comes from cannabis plants with higher THC levels and is often associated with recreational use. This type may be subject to stricter regulations and is generally available in states where cannabis is legal.</p></p>
