<p><p>Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions encompass three main market types: Network DLP, Storage DLP, and Endpoint DLP. Network DLP monitors and controls data in transit across networks, preventing unauthorized transmission. Storage DLP focuses on safeguarding data at rest in databases, file systems, and cloud storage, ensuring sensitive information is protected from breaches. Endpoint DLP secures data on individual devices, preventing unauthorized access or sharing. Together, these solutions provide comprehensive protection against data breaches and loss across various environments.</p></p>
