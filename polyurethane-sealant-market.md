<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane sealant market is primarily divided into two types: single-component and multi-component systems. Single-component sealants are ready to use and cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing several components before application, offering enhanced performance and durability for demanding environments. Each type caters to specific needs, with single-component being suitable for general use and multi-component ideal for specialized industrial applications where strength and flexibility are crucial.</p></p>
