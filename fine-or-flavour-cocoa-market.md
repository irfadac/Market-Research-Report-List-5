<p><p>The fine or flavour cocoa market comprises three primary product types: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor, produced from ground cocoa beans, serves as the base for chocolate and confectionery products. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from cocoa liquor, is essential for texture and creaminess in chocolates and cosmetics. Cocoa powder is created by removing cocoa butter from cocoa liquor, providing a rich flavor for baking and beverages. Together, these products cater to diverse culinary and cosmetic applications.</p></p>
