<p><strong>The Electrocatalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Electrocatalysts</li><li>Heterogeneous Electrocatalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrocatalyst market is primarily categorized into two types: homogeneous and heterogeneous electrocatalysts. Homogeneous electrocatalysts exist in the same phase as the reactants, typically dissolving in a solvent and providing enhanced reaction rates and selectivity. In contrast, heterogeneous electrocatalysts are typically solid materials that facilitate reactions at the interface with liquids or gases, offering advantages such as stability and ease of separation. Both types play crucial roles in energy conversion and storage technologies, including fuel cells and batteries.</p></p>
