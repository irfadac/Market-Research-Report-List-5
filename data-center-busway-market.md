<p><ul><li>3-Phase 4-Wire</li><li>3-Phase 5-Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Busway market features primarily two types: 3-Phase 4-Wire and 3-Phase 5-Wire systems. </p><p>The 3-Phase 4-Wire configuration offers three phases of power distribution and a neutral wire, ideal for balanced loads and supporting standard equipment. </p><p>In contrast, the 3-Phase 5-Wire system includes an additional ground wire, enhancing safety by providing better fault protection. This configuration is commonly used in environments requiring higher power and reliability, making it suitable for more complex data center operations.</p></p>
