<p><p>The image detection sensor market comprises three main types: CCD, CMOS, and thermal sensors. </p><p>CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for professional photography and scientific applications. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors offer lower power consumption and faster processing speeds, suitable for consumer electronics and automotive systems. Thermal sensors detect infrared radiation, enabling temperature measurement and night vision capabilities, commonly used in surveillance, automotive, and industrial applications. Each type serves specialized needs across various industries.</p></p>
