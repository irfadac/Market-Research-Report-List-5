<p><p>The humic-based biostimulants market includes three primary types: humic acid, fulvic acid, and potassium humate. Humic acid enhances soil structure and nutrient retention, promoting plant growth. Fulvic acid, being smaller in molecular size, facilitates nutrient absorption and transportation within plants, improving overall health. Potassium humate combines the benefits of humic substances with potassium, supporting root development and stress resistance. Together, these products contribute to sustainable agriculture by improving soil fertility and promoting efficient nutrient use in crops.</p></p>
