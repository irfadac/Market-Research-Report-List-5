<p><p>The Ammonia Internal Combustion Engine market is categorized into three main types: Spark-Ignition Engines, Compression-Ignition Engines, and Gas-Turbine Engines. Spark-Ignition Engines use a spark plug to ignite the ammonia-air mixture, making them suitable for lighter applications. Compression-Ignition Engines rely on high pressure and temperature to ignite ammonia, providing higher efficiency for heavy-duty uses. Gas-Turbine Engines utilize ammonia in a continuous flow process to generate power, ideal for high-output energy applications, reflecting the growing interest in sustainable fuel alternatives.</p></p>
