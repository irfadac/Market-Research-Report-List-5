<p><strong>The Bilirubin Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diazonium Method</li><li>Vanadate Oxidation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bilirubin Assay Kits market encompasses various methodologies for measuring bilirubin levels in clinical diagnostics. The Diazonium Method utilizes diazonium salts to form a colored compound in the presence of bilirubin, facilitating spectrophotometric measurement. In contrast, the Vanadate Oxidation Method employs vanadate ions to oxidize bilirubin, producing a colorimetric response that can be quantified. Both methods are vital for diagnosing liver dysfunction and hemolytic diseases, with each offering distinct advantages in sensitivity and specificity for bilirubin detection.</p></p>
