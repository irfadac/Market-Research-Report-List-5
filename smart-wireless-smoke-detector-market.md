<p><ul><li>Ionic Smoke Alarm</li><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Wireless Smoke Detector market features two primary types: ionic smoke alarms and photoelectric smoke alarms. Ionic smoke alarms use radioactive material to detect smoke particles and are highly sensitive to fast-flaming fires, making them effective in certain conditions. In contrast, photoelectric smoke alarms utilize a light source and sensor to identify smoke, particularly from smoldering fires, offering quicker detection in those scenarios. Both types are integrated into smart systems for remote monitoring and enhanced safety features.</p></p>
