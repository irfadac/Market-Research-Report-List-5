<p><strong>The Mezcal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Joven</li><li>Reposado</li><li>Anejo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mezcal market is categorized into three main types based on aging processes. Joven, or "young," is unaged and showcases the raw flavor of agave. Reposado is aged in wood barrels for 2 to 12 months, allowing it to develop nuanced flavors while retaining its agave character. Anejo, aged for over a year, offers deeper, richer tastes with complexity from prolonged barrel maturation. Each type targets different consumer preferences, from those seeking vibrant freshness to those desiring refined depth in flavor.</p></p>
