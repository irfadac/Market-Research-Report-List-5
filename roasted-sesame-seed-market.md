<p><p>The roasted sesame seed market is primarily segmented into two types: white roasted sesame seeds and dark roasted sesame seeds. White roasted sesame seeds are made from hulled seeds that undergo light roasting, offering a mild flavor and a crunchy texture, making them popular in salads, snacks, and Asian cuisines. In contrast, dark roasted sesame seeds are made from unhulled seeds that undergo a deeper roasting process, resulting in a richer, bolder flavor, often used in sauces, dips, and gourmet dishes.</p></p>
