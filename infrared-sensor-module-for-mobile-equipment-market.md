<p><p>The Infrared Sensor Module for Mobile Equipment market features two primary types: Uncooled Infrared (UIR) sensors and Cooled Infrared (CIR) sensors. UIR sensors operate at ambient temperatures, making them compact and cost-effective, suitable for applications like thermal imaging in smartphones and automotive systems. In contrast, CIR sensors require cooling mechanisms to detect lower temperature variations with higher sensitivity and accuracy, ideal for specialized applications such as military, surveillance, and industrial processes where precise thermal readings are crucial.</p></p>
