<p><strong>The Ceramic Decal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Decals</li><li>Silkscreen Decals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic decal market features two primary types: digital decals and silkscreen decals. Digital decals utilize advanced printing technology to create intricate designs and patterns directly onto ceramic surfaces. They offer high customization and detail, ideal for unique, one-off designs. Silkscreen decals, on the other hand, involve a traditional screen printing method, where ink is pushed through a mesh screen onto the ceramic. This technique is suited for bulk production, providing vibrant colors and durability, making it popular for mass-market items.</p></p>
