<p><p>Exhaust Gas Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) are essential for measuring vehicle emissions. The market includes two main types: Manual and Automatic systems. Manual CVS, utilizing a single-venturi system, requires operators to manage sampling actively, often leading to variability in results. In contrast, Automatic CVS employs a multi-venturi system, allowing for continuous and precise sampling with minimal human intervention. This automation enhances reliability and data accuracy, making Automatic systems increasingly preferred in emission testing and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
