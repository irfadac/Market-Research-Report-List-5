<p><p>The circuit protection components market primarily includes two types: overcurrent protection components and overvoltage protection components. Overcurrent protection components, such as fuses and circuit breakers, prevent excessive current flow that can cause overheating and damage to electrical circuits. Overvoltage protection components, like voltage suppressors and surge protectors, safeguard equipment from voltage spikes that could lead to failure or degradation. Together, these components are essential for maintaining the safety and reliability of electronic systems across various applications.</p></p>
