<p><p>Impact mills are essential in the processing industry, classified mainly into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal impact mills use a horizontal rotor to deliver high-velocity impacts, suitable for brittle materials and enhancing particle size reduction. In contrast, vertical impact mills feature a vertical rotor configuration, which can achieve finer grinding and is efficient for specific materials, such as chemicals and minerals. Each type targets varied applications, offering unique advantages in processing efficiency and product specifications.</p></p>
