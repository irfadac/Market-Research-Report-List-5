<p><p>The VoIP market can be categorized into three main types. Computer-to-Computer allows users to make voice calls over the internet using software or applications like Skype or Zoom, primarily between PC users. Computer-to-Phone enables users to call traditional phone lines from their computers, often through services like Google Voice. Phone-to-Phone involves direct calls between VoIP phones or devices, allowing for seamless communication without relying on traditional phone networks. Each type serves different communication needs and user preferences.</p></p>
