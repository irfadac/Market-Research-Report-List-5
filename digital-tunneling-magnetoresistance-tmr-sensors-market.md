<p><p>Digital Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) sensors are categorized into three market types: omnipolar, bipolar, and unipolar. </p><p>Omnipolar sensors can detect magnetic fields regardless of their direction, enhancing flexibility in applications. Bipolar sensors respond to both north and south magnetic poles, allowing for more precise positioning. Unipolar sensors, on the other hand, activate only in the presence of a magnetic field of a specific polarity. These distinctions cater to various applications in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics markets.</p></p>
