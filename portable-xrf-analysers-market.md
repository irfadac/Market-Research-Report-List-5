<p><strong>The Portable XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PIN</li><li>SDD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable XRF (X-ray fluorescence) analyzers are categorized primarily into two types based on their detector technology: PIN (P-type Silicon Detector) and SDD (Silicon Drift Detector). PIN detectors are cost-effective and provide adequate sensitivity for various applications, making them suitable for basic analyses. In contrast, SDD detectors offer higher resolution and faster detection times, enhancing the precision and efficiency of element analysis. Both types are widely used in fields such as environmental monitoring, mining, and recycling due to their portability and rapid results.</p></p>
