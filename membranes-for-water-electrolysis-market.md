<p><strong>The Membranes for Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Exchange Membranes</li><li>Ion Exchange Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membranes used in water electrolysis are crucial for efficient hydrogen production. Proton Exchange Membranes (PEM) facilitate the movement of protons while being impermeable to gases, making them suitable for high-efficiency applications and compact systems. Ion Exchange Membranes, on the other hand, allow for the selective transfer of ions but can vary in permeability and cost. Both types play essential roles in electrolysis systems, impacting performance, durability, and overall hydrogen generation efficiency.</p></p>
