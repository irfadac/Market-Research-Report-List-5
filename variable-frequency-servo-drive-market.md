<p><p>The Variable Frequency Servo Drive (VFSD) market is primarily categorized into Low Voltage and Medium Voltage segments. Low Voltage VFSDs typically operate under 1,000 volts and are widely used in applications like HVAC, conveyor systems, and pumps, offering efficient control for smaller motors. In contrast, Medium Voltage VFSDs, operating between 1,000 to 10,000 volts, cater to larger industrial applications such as heavy machinery and electric propulsion systems. Both types enhance energy efficiency and improve performance by allowing precise motor speed control.</p></p>
