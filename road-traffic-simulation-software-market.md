<p><strong>The Road Traffic Simulation Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro-level Traffic Simulation</li><li>Macro-level Traffic Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road traffic simulation software can be categorized into micro-level and macro-level traffic simulation. Micro-level traffic simulation focuses on individual vehicle movements and interactions at the intersection or street level, allowing detailed analysis of traffic flow, driver behavior, and intersection design. In contrast, macro-level traffic simulation examines larger areas or networks, assessing overall traffic flow patterns, congestion, and infrastructure performance. Both types are integral for urban planning, enhancing traffic management strategies, improving safety, and optimizing transportation systems.</p></p>
