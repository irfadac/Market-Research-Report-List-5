<p><p>The Human Rabies Vaccine market comprises several types based on the cell substrates used for vaccine production. Vero cell vaccines are derived from African green monkey kidney cells and are widely utilized for their safety and effectiveness. BHK (Baby Hamster Kidney) vaccines offer another alternative, known for their reliability. Chick embryo cell vaccines, developed from chicken embryos, are traditional options. Human diploid cell vaccines are created from human fibroblast cells, ensuring lower risk of adverse reactions. Other vaccines may involve novel or less common substrates.</p></p>
